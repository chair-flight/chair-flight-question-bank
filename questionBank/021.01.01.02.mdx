2. **021.01.01.02.02** -- Explain that all aircraft are certified according to
   specifications determined by the competent authority, and that these
   certification specifications cover aspects such as design, material quality
   and build quality.
3. **021.01.01.02.03** -- State that the certification specifications for
   aeroplanes issued by EASA are: CS-23 for Normal, Utility, Aerobatic and
   Commuter Aeroplanes; CS-25 for Large Aeroplanes.

   rotorcraft issued by EASA are: CS-27 for Small Rotorcraft; CS-29 for Large
   Rotorcraft.

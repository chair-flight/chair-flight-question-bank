# Starting malfunctions

## Learning Objectives

1. **021.11.04.02.01** -- Describe the indications and the possible causes of the
   following aeroplane starting malfunctions: false (dry or wet) start; tailpipe
   fire (torching); hot start; abortive (hung) start; no N1 rotation; no FADEC
   indications.
2. **021.11.04.02.02** -- Describe the indications and the possible causes of the
   following helicopter starting malfunctions: false (dry or wet) start;
   tailpipe fire (torching); hot start; abortive (hung) start; no N1 rotation;
   freewheel failure; no FADEC indications.

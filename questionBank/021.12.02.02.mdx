# Fire detection

## Learning Objectives

1. **021.12.02.02.01** -- Explain the following principles of fire detection:
   resistance and capacitance; gas pressure.
2. **021.12.02.02.02** -- Explain fire-detection applications such as: bimetallic;
   continuous loop; gaseous loop (gas-filled detectors).
3. **021.12.02.02.03** -- Explain why generally double-loop systems are used.
4. **021.12.02.02.04** -- Give an example of warnings, indications and function
   tests of a fire-protection system.

1. **021.13.01.01.01** -- Describe the basic operating principle of a cockpit
   oxygen system and describe the following different modes of operation: normal
   (diluter demand); 100 per cent; emergency.
2. **021.13.01.01.02** -- Describe the operating principle and the purposes of the
   following two portable oxygen systems: smoke hood; portable bottle.
3. **021.13.01.01.03** -- Describe the following two oxygen systems that can be
   used to supply oxygen to passengers: fixed system (chemical oxygen generator
   or gaseous system); portable.
4. **021.13.01.01.04** -- Describe the actuation methods (automatic and manual)
   and the functioning of a passenger oxygen mask.
5. **021.13.01.01.05** -- Compare chemical oxygen generators to gaseous systems
   with respect to: capacity; flow regulation.
6. **021.13.01.01.06** -- State the dangers of grease or oil related to the use of
   oxygen systems.

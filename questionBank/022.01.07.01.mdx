# Torque, torquemeters

## Learning Objectives

1. [022.01.07.01.01] -- Define "torque".
2. [022.01.07.01.02] -- Explain the relationship between power, torque and rpm.
3. [022.01.07.01.03] -- List the following units used for torque: Newton meters;
   inch or foot pounds.
4. [022.01.07.01.04] -- State that engine torque can be displayed as a
   percentage.
5. [022.01.07.01.05] -- List and describe the following different types of
   torquemeters, and explain their operating principles: mechanical; electronic.
6. [022.01.07.01.06] -- Compare the two systems with regard to design and
   weight.
7. [022.01.07.01.07] -- Give examples of display.

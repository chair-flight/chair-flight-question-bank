# Gyroscope: basic principles

## Learning Objectives

1. **022.04.01.01.01** -- Define a "gyro".
2. **022.04.01.01.02** -- Explain the fundamentals of the theory of gyroscopic
   forces.
3. **022.04.01.01.03** -- Define the "degrees of freedom" of a gyro. Remark: As
   a convention, the degrees of freedom of a gyroscope do not include its own
   axis of rotation (the spin axis).
4. **022.04.01.01.04** -- Explain the following terms: rigidity; precession;
   wander (drift/topple).
5. **022.04.01.01.05** -- Explain the three types of gyro wander:

- real wander;
- apparent wander;
- transport wander.

6. **022.04.01.01.06** -- Describe the two ways of driving gyroscopes and any
   associated indications:
   - air/vacuum;
   - electrically.

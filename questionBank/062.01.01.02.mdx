# Frequency, wavelength, amplitude, phase angle

## Learning Objectives

1. **062.01.01.02.01** -- Define "frequency": the number of cycles occurring in
   1 second expressed in Hertz (Hz).
2. **062.01.01.02.02** -- Define "wavelength": the physical distance travelled
   by a radio wave during one cycle of transmission.
3. **062.01.01.02.03** -- Define "amplitude": the maximum deflection in an
   oscillation or wave.
4. **062.01.01.02.04** -- State that the relationship between wavelength and
   frequency is: wavelength (lamda) = speed of light (c) / frequency (f).
5. **062.01.01.02.05** -- Define "phase angle": the fraction of one wavelength
   expressed in degrees from 000 degrees to 360 degrees .
6. **062.01.01.02.06** -- Define "phase angle difference/shift": the angular
   difference between the corresponding points of two cycles of equal
   wavelength, which is measurable in degrees.

# Presentation and interpretation

## Learning Objectives

1. [062.02.02.02.01] -- Name the types of indicators commonly in use: electronic
   display; radio magnetic indicator (RMI); fixed-card ADF (radio compass);
   moving-card ADF.
2. [062.02.02.02.02] -- Interpret the indications given on RMI, fixed-card and
   moving-card ADF displays.
3. [062.02.02.02.03] -- Given a display, interpret the relevant ADF information.
4. [062.02.02.02.04] -- Calculate the true bearing from the compass heading and
   relative bearing.
5. [062.02.02.02.05] -- Convert the compass bearing into magnetic bearing and
   true bearing.
6. [062.02.02.02.06] -- Describe how to fly the following in-flight ADF
   procedures: homing and tracking, and explain the influence of wind;
   interception of inbound QDM and outbound QDR; changing from one QDM/QDR to
   another; determining station passage and the abeam point.
